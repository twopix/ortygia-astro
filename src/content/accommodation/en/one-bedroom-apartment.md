  Bright and airy, the apartment combines Cycladic architecture with modern design, offering a fully equipped kitchenette, a dining area, and a stylish bathroom with a hydromassage shower.

  Outdoors, guests can relax on their private patio with a pergola, enjoying beautiful views of the village of Ano Mera — the perfect setting for morning coffee or a quiet evening under the Mykonian sky.

  Blending elegance, tranquility, and authentic island charm, this apartment is an ideal choice for those seeking a peaceful stay close to Mykonos’ best beaches and local life.
